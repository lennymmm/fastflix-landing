Privacy Policy

FastFlix - Your privacy is our priority

Last updated: November 2025

At FastFlix, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains the information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information Collected

1.1 Information You Provide

  • User Account: If you choose to register, we collect your email address
  • Preferences: Your favorite genres, selected streaming platforms
  • Saved Content: Movies and series you mark as favorites

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Interactions with the app, content viewed
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifier
  • Performance Data: Information about crashes and errors to improve the app

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization: To show you relevant content based on your preferences
  • Synchronization: To synchronize your preferences between devices (if you have an account)
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage and improve app functionality
  • Communications: To send you important service notifications
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized use

3. Third-Party Services

FastFlix uses the following third-party services:

3.1 Supabase

  • User authentication
  • Cloud storage for preferences
  • Gestión de suscripciones premium

3.2 Google AdMob

  • Personalized ads (free version)
  • Can collect device identifiers
  • You can manage ad preferences in your device settings

3.3 The Movie Database (TMDb)

  • Movies and series data
  • Images and trailers
  • We do not share your personal data with TMDb

3.4 YouTube

  • Trailers playback
  • Subject to YouTube/Google privacy policy

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information in these cases:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, security, or property
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure storage on Supabase servers
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security audits

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request the deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Opposition: Opt out of marketing communications
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data

7. Data Retention

  • We keep your data while your account is active
  • When you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days
  • Anonymous data may be retained for statistical analysis

8. Children's Privacy

FastFlix is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • Notification in the app
  • Email (if you have an account)
  • Update of the "Last Updated" date above

10. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our app does not use cookies. However, third-party services (like AdMob) may use similar technologies for:

  • Personalizing ads
  • Performance analysis
  • Improving user experience

11. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure that these transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

12. Contact

To exercise your rights or if you have questions about this policy, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@fastflix.app
  • Web: https://fastflix.app

We will respond to your request within 30 days.
