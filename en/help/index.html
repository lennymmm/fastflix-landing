‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No, you can use FastFlix without creating an account. The app works with an anonymous session that automatically saves your preferences, lists and likes in the cloud.

In the future, you can create an account to access additional features such as public profile, advanced statistics and follow other users.

How d oes synchronization work?

Your lists, preferences and likes are synchronized automatically in the cloud using Supabase. This means that:

  • Your data is secure and backed up
  • You can access from any device (coming soon)
  • You don't have to worry about losing your information
Where are my data stored?

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database platform. We use an anonymous session, so you don't need to provide personal information.

The data includes: content in your list, content watched, likes, and platform preferences.

Is FastFlix free?

Yes, FastFlix is completely free. We show ads to keep the service running.

In the future, we will offer a premium version without ads with additional features.

Can I use FastFlix offline?

Currently FastFlix requires internet connection to function, as it consults updated information about movies, series and streaming platforms.

We are working on a basic offline mode that will allow you to view your list and saved content without connection.

How is the content updated?

The movie and series catalog is regularly updated from TMDb (The Movie Database), one of the most complete content databases in the world.

Streaming platform information is periodically updated to reflect current availability.

What streaming platforms are supported?

FastFlix supports the main streaming platforms:

  • Netflix
  • Amazon Prime Video
  • Disney+
  • HBO Max
  • Apple TV+
  • Movistar+
  • And more...

We are constantly adding new platforms according to user demand.

Can I change the app language?

Currently FastFlix is available in English and Spanish. More languages will be available in future updates.
