About FastFlix app

FastFlix is an app that helps you discover where to watch movies and series on legal streaming platforms. It shows you which services each title is available on and helps you organize what you want to watch.

FastFlix does not host or stream video content and does not offer pirated movies or series. We are not affiliated with any websites that provide illegal streaming; our mission is to help you find content on Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, HBO Max and other official platforms.
